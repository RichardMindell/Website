About

I graduated from the University of Bristol in July 2022 with First Class Honours in Computer Science. Throughout my time at university I studied various disciplines across Computer Science from machine learning to interaction design. This has provided me with a strong theoretical background for software development and analytics.

My dissertation explored electric scooters in Bristol and their safety through a user-centred design lens. This was an unconventional approach in the normally rigid structure of computer science. From my HCI based projects at university, I learnt that looking at users and understanding their behaviour is just as important as proficiency in mathematics or coding.

I hope to take the skillset that I have developed so far into my future career. Furthermore, I am eager to improve upon it within a supporting yet challenging environment.

The website was built from scratch using JavaScript, HTML and CSS by me.
